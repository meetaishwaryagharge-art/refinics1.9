Mortgage Loans

A Mortgage Loan, commonly known as a Loan Against Property (LAP), is a smart and efficient way to unlock the value of your residential or commercial property to meet important personal or business financial requirements without selling the asset.

Being a secured form of borrowing, mortgage loans offer lower interest rates compared to unsecured loans. Additionally, leading banks and NBFCs provide longer repayment tenures, often extending up to 15 years or 20 years in certain cases, ensuring comfortable EMIs and better cash-flow management.

For entrepreneurs and business owners, Mortgage Loan Overdraft facilities offer enhanced flexibility allowing access to funds as and when required, with interest charged only on the amount utilised.

At REFINICS Consultants, we assist you in securing well-structured mortgage loan solutions from leading banks and NBFCs, carefully aligned to your financial profile, funding needs, and long-term objectives. Our approach focuses on clarity, transparency, and value, ensuring you receive the most competitive terms with a seamless borrowing experience.

Balance Transfer

Optimising your existing borrowings through a loan balance transfer can significantly enhance your financial efficiency. By transferring your loans to a more suitable financial institution, you may benefit from reduced interest rates, lower EMIs, extended repayment tenures, and the possibility of additional top-up funding to meet evolving needs.

Balance transfer of secured loans or working capital facilities also enables consolidation of multiple liabilities into a streamlined structure. This not only simplifies cash flow management but also helps reduce the overall cost of funds over time.

With most leading banks offering specialised balance transfer schemes, identifying the right opportunity requires careful evaluation. At REFNICS Consultants, we provide expert guidance to assess your eligibility, compare lender offerings, and manage the entire transition with clarity and precision ensuring your financing structure remains aligned with your long-term objectives.

Unsecured Business Loans

Unsecured Business Loans provide businesses with access to capital without the need to pledge any asset or property as collateral. These loans are evaluated purely on the strength of your financial performance, credit profile, and repayment capability.

Designed to support business expansion, growth initiatives, and operational requirements, unsecured loans are offered by banks and NBFCs based on clearly defined end-use of funds. Since no security is involved, these loans typically carry higher interest rates and shorter tenures compared to secured financing.

At REFNICS Consultants, we specialise in structuring Unsecured Business Loan solutions that align with your business objectives. Leveraging our strong network of partner banks and NBFCs, we help you secure the most suitable financing with transparent terms, competitive pricing, and a streamlined approval process, so you can focus on growing your business with confidence.

Working Capital Financing

Efficient cash flow is the backbone of every successful business. Working Capital Financing provides short-term funding support to manage day-to-day operational requirements such as salaries, rent, vendor payments, inventory procurement, and receivables management to SMEs and Corporates. Unlike long-term loans meant for asset creation, working capital facilities are designed to ensure continuity, liquidity, and operational stability.

REFINICS Consultants facilitates a wide spectrum of working capital solutions through its network of leading banks and NBFC partners. Our offerings include Cash Credit, Bank Overdrafts, Working Capital Term Loans (WCTL), Drop Line Overdrafts (DLOD), Bill / Invoice Discounting, Bank Guarantees and other structured working capital products carefully aligned with your business cycle and cash flow patterns.

At REFINICS, our approach is advisory led and solution driven. We work closely with businesses to assess funding needs, structure optimal limits, and negotiate competitive terms ensuring transparency, flexibility, and long-term value. With our deep lender relationships and process expertise, we help businesses maintain financial agility and focus confidently on growth.
